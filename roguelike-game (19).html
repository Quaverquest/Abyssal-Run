📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎮 MOVEMENT

AD or ←→ to move. W or SPACE to jump. Jumping is essential — platforms are everywhere.

⚔ COMBAT

LMB (left click) to attack. Your character swings in the direction they're facing. Tap rapidly for maximum damage output. RMB activates your first ability — powerful but costs MP.

💨 DASH

E to dash in your movement direction. Dashing grants brief invincibility — use it to dodge through enemy attacks. Short cooldown, use it often.

🪙 GOLD & SHOP

Every enemy drops gold scaled to their danger. Goblins: 8g · Demons: 35g · Warlords: 80g · Mega Bosses: 300–800g. After clearing each floor, a shop opens. Spend gold on HP, damage, abilities, and more. Gold carries over — save up for expensive upgrades!

⭐ PROGRESSION

Enemies drop gold. Spend it at the shop to buy weapons, armor upgrades, HP, and abilities after each floor. Gold carries over between floors — save up for powerful gear.

💀 MEGA BOSSES

After 10 minutes of play, a Mega Boss invades the next floor. They have multiple phases, getting faster and more aggressive as you damage them. Watch the HUD for a countdown. Boss kills reward massive gold.

🏪 EVERY 5 FLOORS

On floors 5, 10, 15… the shop unlocks extra tabs: Abilities (buy new skills for gold), Appearance (change your skin mid-run), and Controls (rebind any key). Explore all tabs!

🔑 SECRET CODES

There's a Codes tab in the shop. Codes are earned through in-game milestones — keep playing, reach new floors, defeat bosses, and you'll unlock them naturally. The game won't tell you what they are... discover them yourself.

💾 SAVE FILES

You have 3 save slots. Each slot remembers your high score, deepest floor, unlocked codes, and skin preferences. Your progress is saved automatically and backed up to the browser cache.
